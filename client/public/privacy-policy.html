Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 29, 2025

Welcome to xCHnG. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use xCHnG, we may collect the following information:

  • Registration Information: Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Locality, Password.
  • Documents: Files you upload to the platform (stored securely on Firebase).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for purposes including:

  • To verify and approve registrations (admin approval required).
  • To allow users to upload and download documents (subject to admin approval).
  • To provide customer support and improve our services.
  • To ensure security and prevent unauthorized access.

3. Data Storage and Security

Uploaded documents are stored on Firebase Storage. User data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas. We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (for file storage)
  • MongoDB Atlas (for database)
  • Vercel (frontend hosting)
  • Render (backend hosting)

We are not responsible for the practices of these third parties, and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • Your data may only be shared with admin(s) for approval of registrations or file access.
  • We may disclose information if required by law.

6. Data Retention

Registration details and uploaded documents are stored until you request deletion or your account is terminated. You can request deletion of your account and related data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
